About Us

We're a team of creatives and tech enthusiasts who saw the need for a mobile, comfortable, and professional-quality green screen solution — and we made it happen!

Our goal is to help you create high-quality content from anywhere without the bulk of traditional equipment.

Founded in 2023, Green Screen Blanket Co. has quickly become the go-to solution for content creators, remote workers, and streamers who need flexibility without compromising quality.

Our Story

2022

The Idea

During the remote work boom, our founder Sherwin Lloyd struggled with virtual backgrounds during important meetings. Traditional green screens were bulky and inconvenient.

2023

Development

After months of testing different materials and designs, we created the perfect portable green screen that doubled as a comfortable blanket.

2024

Launch

We launched our product to rave reviews from content creators and remote workers alike.

2025

Growth

Today, we're expanding our product line and reaching customers worldwide who love the convenience and quality of our green screen blankets.
