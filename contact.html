Frequently Asked Questions

Paano lilinisin ang green screen blanket?

Machine wash cold on gentle cycle. Tumble dry on low heat or hang to dry. Do not use bleach.

Anong software ang mainam na gamitin sa greenscreen niyo?

Our green screen blanket works with all major video conferencing and streaming software including Zoom, OBS, Streamlabs, Skype, and more.

How do I get the best results with my green screen?

Ensure even lighting across the green surface, avoid wrinkles, and position yourself at least 2 feet away from the screen for best results.

Do you ship internationally?

Yes po! We ship to most countries worldwide. Shipping rates and delivery times vary by location.

Ano yung inyong return policy?

We offer a 30-day satisfaction guarantee. If you're not happy with your purchase, you can return it for a full refund.
